What is Programming?

Programming is the process of designing, writing, testing, and maintaining computer programs. It involves using programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++ to create software applications, websites, and more.

Why Learn Programming?

Learning how to code opens up many possibilities for your career and personal life. You can build your own apps or websites, automate tasks, or even work as a software developer or web designer. It's also a valuable skill to have in today's digital world.
